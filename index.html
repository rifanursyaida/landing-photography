About Me

I'm a documentary photographer based in Indonesia, passionate about capturing raw, unfiltered moments that reveal the depth of human experience. My work spans across urban landscapes and remote villages throughout the archipelago.

About Portfolio

My portfolio represents years of fieldwork across the Indonesian archipelago — from the fishing communities of Java to the highland traditions of Sulawesi — always searching for the decisive, unrepeatable moment.

About Me

My Vision

To create photographs that transcend mere documentation — images that provoke empathy, spark conversation, and bear witness to stories that might otherwise go unseen. Every frame is a conversation between light, subject, and truth.

My Mission

To dedicate my craft to communities whose stories deserve amplification. I believe photography is a responsibility — to the subjects, to the audience, and to the historical record. My mission is to document with dignity and purpose.
